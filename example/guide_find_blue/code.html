CORE CONCEPT

What is Mismatch Score?

The Mismatch Score measures the gap between current business density and latent consumer demand in a specific micro-location. A higher score indicates a "Blue Ocean"—an underserved market with significant growth potential.

Low (0-30)

Saturated market. High competition.

Med (31-70)

Stable demand. Moderate barriers.

High (71-100)

Prime Opportunity. High demand.
